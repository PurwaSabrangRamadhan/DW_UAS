Pancasila University
Information System

SIUP is an android application that allows you to access several menus available at Pancasila University

Get it on - Google Play

Feature

  • Today's Lecture Schedule
  • Academic Information System (SIAK)
  • Financial Information System (SIKEU)
  • E - Library
  • Admission of New Students (PMB)
  • Lecturer Data at Pancasila University
  • Alumni Data at Pancasila University
  • E - Learning
  • Lecturer Research Data
  • Official Website
  • Graduation Info
